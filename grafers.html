Week 1

Convincing the buyer

Our product is said to be completely different than others in many means, by our testers. You must have seen experiments showing the number of preservatives, synthetic chemicals, and fats in other wafers. We took it upon ourselves to prepare a wafer that is equally satisfying to eat, and still is healthy for the bodys digestive system. Not only this, but a considerable amount of the profit is donated to the charity for the benefit of physically ill people, especially those who suffer from serious obesity. We, here at Grafers, assure you that all types of safety measures are practiced to make these wafers that would not only make you crave for more, but keep you healthy and fit. Our wafers are very different than others in shape, because we do not use chemicals and harmful preservatives, but they are composed completely of natural vegetables and organic materials. The spice of mothers cooking, of course, is still there.

Week 2

Convincing the
Small Shops VS. Supermarkets

Small Retailers :

We would be sure to provide them a higher margin of sale than other products. A new product would surely catch more eyes. Our product comes in a very wide variety of flavours, so that no one would miss out. Some examples are Chilli Cheese, Italian Peri Peri, Onion, Salted, Mint, Mexican, and a few more. All of these would be sent to you by us as per your wish. As the product is very unique in style and pleasing for customers, it is bound to attract a lot of people towards itself to atleast try it. As we already specified the qualities of our product in the last week report, you will have surely got the idea of how our product will naturally attract customers.
Offers like a 1gb internet coupon free per packet, are going to be released in our next week report. This offer would be very beneficial and appeasing to the retailer and the customer respectively. More people will be attracted towards this product as everyone would like free data.

Wholesalers:

Here is an example. Think if 100 wafers are sold. A 1000 rupees would be gathered. Now, the wholesaler will be getting them from us for 800 rupees. So, the profit margin is pretty good just now, and once the product gets enough popularity ( which, surely will be very soon ), the wholesaler who has taken the dealership now, will be in great demand later.

Week 3

Collaboration with investors

We have finally collaborated with a major internet provider, Gitel. As a very popular and wide known brand, a collaboration with gitel would automatically turn peoples attention towards our product.
This, plus our exceptional brand casting, will make our product any investors fantasy. Just as gitel grabbed their chance, so should you. Sponsoring us just isnt cheaper than most products, but we would also provide the most beneficial deals in return.
So, rest assured you wont be put into loss, or be thrown into despair. Not only would you spread your name to any customers who dont know about your company, but you can keep offers that would benefit the customers and keep them craving for more.
A perfect example would be gitels offer. They are offering 1gb data per packet. This would not only increase their popularity and show their quality, but also benefit the customers. Who wouldnt like a GB data free, while also savouring an absolutely mouth-watering wafer? So, sponsors could keep this type of offers that would keep the customers coming back.
As you already know about the qualities of our wafer, we have started receiving huge offers for sponsorships. Ours is a preposterously unique wafer by every angle, be it with the advertising or the health sector. We confidently boast of being healthy for the digestive system, while being a little too delicious for the taste buds.
So, what are you waiting for, contact us at our business email for sponsorships! Any customer would love to not only eat the wafer, but also enjoy offers from other sponsors.
We are planning our first advertisement soon! So dont waste any time and contact us asap for being featured on the packet!

Week 4

Summary and launch of the product.

So, the day arrived. The very fantasy of our team, the day that beholded our hopes, the day that would change everything in our lives, everything in others life as well, the day that would either result in us celebrating, or weeping. All of this was depended on the public. And boy did they respond as energetically as we would have thought in our wildest dreams. The day came. And it went. Just like any other day. But the only difference was that it changed everything.
The restless sweating, the endless ticking of the clock, all the work in the past few weeks, was to be given an official recognition. All our hard work was to be given its result on that day. However well we advertised, however well we boasted of our products qualities, it would all depend on the publics taste. Our product was quite different from others. It was a risk even to think about trying to execute this. It could end up being a boon or a curse. Yet, here we are. In front of everyone.
The sheer suddenness of the launch vanished, and the day ended with tears of joy, celebration of kinds, unity of the team, and the happiness that nothing else could give us.
The success of Grafers has been preposterously exceptional. In one day, Grafers topped the market as quickly and as broadly then anyone could have ever imagined.
Gitel, our biggest sponsor, ringed us just as the launch day drew to a close, and said it had been the most important day of their life when they singed their deal with us.
It is still a pang to think of what would have happened if the public hadnt liked our wafer. But we won't talk about that. The public welcomed Grafers with open arms. The market was absolutely astounded with a wafer that topped the market in just a day.
It is really great to think that, Hard work at its best form is when it pays off. And that, really, what was pinned in our minds whenever we were tired. Our brains were completely racked thinking of how to make our wafer different. But even if we accomplish getting a sufficient plan, it doesnt guarantee that the public will like a different taste.
So, our biggest Thank You goes to you guys. Thanks for helping us grow and we welcome even more opportunities, more flavours, more sponsors, more effort, and above all, more support. So, this was our success story.
From scratching out the wafer packet in a single piece of paper, to being the best wafer on the market, we have experienced it all. Again, thanks to the public and keep munching on our wafers!
